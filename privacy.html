Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Welcome to ShareShift. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how ShareShift ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and manages your information when you use our multi-tenant social media automation and content generation platform.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and business details provided during registration.
  • Third-Party Credentials (OAuth Tokens): To automate posts on your behalf, we store encrypted access and refresh tokens for services such as Google Business Profile and Meta (Facebook/Instagram).
  • Content Data: Text, images, and scheduling data you input into the platform for the purpose of social media publication.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our platform to improve user experience and system performance.

2. Use of Third-Party APIs (Google & Meta)

ShareShift's core functionality relies on integration with Google Business Profile and Meta Graph APIs. By connecting your accounts, you acknowledge the following:

  • Purpose: We use these APIs exclusively to manage business listings, publish posts, and retrieve performance metrics as requested by you.
  • Data Handling: We do not store your raw login credentials. We only use secure OAuth tokens.
  • Compliance: Our use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and optimize our automation services.
  • Generate and publish content to your authorized social media profiles.
  • Provide analytics and performance reports for your business locations.
  • Communicate with you regarding service updates or technical support.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Data is only shared in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We may share data with trusted cloud infrastructure providers (e.g., GCP, Oracle Cloud) solely to host our services.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including AES-256 encryption for all stored API tokens and SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit. As a SaaS provider, we prioritize the isolation of data between different client tenants to ensure your business information remains private.

6. Your Rights (POPIA & GDPR)

Consistent with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) and General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request the correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request the deletion of your account and all associated API tokens.
  • Revoke ShareShift's access to your Google or Meta accounts at any time via your respective platform security settings.
